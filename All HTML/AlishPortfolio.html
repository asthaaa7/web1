Projects

  • Web Based Aplication

    One of my proudest projects as an IT computing student was the development of a web-based
    application for a local small business. The application aimed to automate their daily tasks and
    improve efficiency. I used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP to build a user-friendly interface
    and linked it to a MySQL database to securely store and retrieve data. The end result was a
    seamless system that streamlines the business's workflow and saves them time and resources.
    This project allowed me to apply my technical skills and creativity to solve a real-world
    problem, and I am confident that it will make a valuable addition to my portfolio.

  • Mobile App

    Another exciting project I worked on as an IT computing student was the creation of a mobile app
    for a local community event. The app provided real-time updates, interactive maps, and a social
    network for attendees to connect and engage with one another. I utilized my knowledge of Java
    and Android Studio to develop the app and integrated features such as push notifications,
    geolocation, and camera integration. The app was well received by the community and received
    positive feedback for its user-friendly design and robust functionality. This project gave me
    the opportunity to showcase my mobile app development skills and I am proud to include it in my portfolio.
