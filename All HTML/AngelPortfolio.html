My Projects

  • Network Security

    I collaborated with a team of IT students to create a network security system for a simulated
    small business. Our goal was to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of
    the company's data. We implemented various security measures such as firewalls, intrusion
    detection systems, and encryption algorithms. Additionally, we conducted penetration testing
    to identify and address any potential security threats. This project allowed me to gain
    practical experience in network security and exposed me to the importance of proactive
    measures to protect against cyber attacks. It demonstrates my ability to work in a team
    and apply my technical skills to real-world problems, making it a valuable addition to my portfolio.

  • Chatbox

    Another project as an IT computing student, I developed a chatbot for a local tourist information center.
    The chatbot was designed to assist visitors in finding information about nearby attractions,
    restaurants, and accommodations. I used natural language processing techniques and built
    the chatbot using Dialogflow and integrated it with a website. The chatbot was able to understand
    and respond to a variety of questions, providing personalized recommendations and
    information to visitors. This project allowed me to gain experience in developing conversational
    interfaces and working with NLP technologies. I am excited to include this project in
    my portfolio as it highlights my ability to create practical and user-friendly technology solutions.
